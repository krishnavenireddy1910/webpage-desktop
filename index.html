About Starbucks

Starbucks is a global American multinational chain of coffeehouses, roasteries, and coffee brands, headquartered in Seattle, Washington, founded in 1971 by Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker as a coffee bean wholesaler. Led by Howard Schultz, it transformed into a coffee shop chain, expanding aggressively to become a dominant global brand. The company serves a wide range of coffee, tea, pastries, and other food products in over 38,000 stores across 80 markets worldwide, focusing on providing quality products, a welcoming environment, and a positive community impact through its employees, known as "partners".
